Notice:

White water rafting is a thrilling activity, however it is important to be aware of the dangers involved. Dangers present while white water rafting include the risk of drowning, head injuries, hypothermia, and back injuries. While these may seem serious, we do everything we possibly can to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone. By agreeing to book with us, you agree to take upon yourself or the rest of your party, the risks involved with white water rafting. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us.
